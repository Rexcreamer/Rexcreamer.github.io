Membership Level

Membership Level Benefits:

NP Membership

  • For non profit organizations and there is no fee

Bronze Membership

  • 15% discount on tourney entry fees
  • Have a bronze medal next to your name in the discord.
  • Have access in participating in small tourneys.
  • Create a team of 10 people to use for different games. (Up to 5 members could join in the same tourney)
  • Fee of $5/month

Silver Membership

  • 25% discount on tourney entry fees
  • You can start 1 small tourney every 3 months
  • Have access to 1 dedicated trainer.
  • Have access in participating in small tourneys.
  • Have a silver medal next to your name in the discord.
  • Create a team of 25 people to use for different games. (Up to 5 members could join in the same tourney)
  • Fee of $10/month

Gold Membership

  • 50% discount on tourney entry fees
  • You can start small tourneys.
  • Have a gold medal next to your name in the discord.
  • Have access in participating in small tourneys.
  • Create a team of 50 people to use for different games. (Up to 5 members could join in the same tourney)
  • Have access to dedicated trainers.
  • Fee of $15/month
